# PPGCosmo

**International PhD Program in Astrophysics, Cosmology and Gravitation**  
Universidade Federal do Espírito Santo (Ufes) · Vitória, Brazil

[ppgcosmo.github.io](https://ppgcosmo.github.io) 

---

PPGCosmo is an international doctoral program bringing together faculty,
postdoctoral researchers, and students from institutions in Brazil and abroad.
Research spans theoretical and observational astrophysics, cosmology, and
gravitation, with participation in major collaborations.
